Privacy Policy

Customer Service Guy - Last updated May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Customer Service Guy handles candidate information when people use the Android app to prepare an email-based referral application for customer service opportunities in Lisbon, Portugal.

Customer Service Guy does not upload CVs to Firebase or to any app-controlled server. The app opens the user's email app with the selected CV attached, and the user must send the email manually.

Developer and contact

Customer Service Guy is operated by Algorise. For privacy questions, data requests, or deletion requests, contact: algorise@usa.com.

Information the app handles

The app may handle the following information during use:

  • The selected referral language, such as German or Dutch.
  • Eligibility confirmations selected by the candidate.
  • An English CV or resume file chosen by the candidate.
  • Information contained in the candidate's CV, such as name, contact details, work history, education, and skills.

The app does not ask the candidate to type their name, email address, or phone number into the app form. Those details may still be included inside the CV or in the email sent by the candidate's email app.

How information is sent

When the candidate taps the send application button, the app opens an email app on the device with a prepared message addressed to algorise@usa.com. The selected CV is attached to that email. The candidate must review and send the email before any information is received by Algorise.

How information is used

Candidate information is used only for recruitment-related purposes:

  • Reviewing basic language and work-eligibility requirements.
  • Preparing or submitting a referral for customer service roles.
  • Communicating with the candidate about the referral process.
  • Supporting screening, interviews, and hiring steps.

Sharing

Candidate information may be shared with recruitment or hiring teams only when needed for referral, screening, interview, and hiring purposes. Customer Service Guy does not sell candidate data and does not share it for advertising.

Storage and retention

The app does not store CVs in an app-controlled database or cloud storage service. Any CV sent by the candidate is stored in the recipient email inbox and any recruitment systems where the referral is processed.

Candidate information is kept only for as long as reasonably needed for recruitment, referral, communication, legal, or record-keeping purposes. A candidate can request deletion by emailing algorise@usa.com.

Security

The app uses the Android system file picker to let the candidate select a CV file and passes that file to the chosen email app. The security of the final email delivery depends on the candidate's email provider and email app. Algorise limits access to received candidate information to people who need it for recruitment-related purposes.

Children

Customer Service Guy is intended for job candidates and is not directed to children under 13. Children under 13 must not submit CVs or personal information through the app.

Third-party services

The app does not use Firebase, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or an app-controlled backend for CV submission. The candidate's chosen email provider may process the email and attachment according to that provider's own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes or when legal, operational, or Google Play requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page.
